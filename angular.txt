Differentiate between Observables and Promises.

Observables are lazy, which means nothing happens until a subscription is made. Whereas Promises are eager; which means as soon as a promise is created, 
the execution takes place. Observable is a stream in which passing of zero or more events is possible and the callback is called for each event. Whereas, 
promise handles a single event.
